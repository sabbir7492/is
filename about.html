About The Authors

Let's Take a Flash Look About The Authors

ERIC CHAISSON

Astrophysicist, ( Harvard University )

Eric holds a doctorate in astrophysics from Harvard University, where he spent 10 years on the faculty of Arts and sciences. For more than two decades thereafter, he served on the senior science staff at the Space Telescope Science Institute and held various professorships at Johns Hopkins and Tufts university.

STEVE MCMILLAN

Astrophysicist, Mathematician ( Cambridge University and Harvard University )

Steve holds a bachelor's and master's degree in mathematics from Cambridge University and doctorate in astronomy from Harvard University. He held post-doctoral position at the University of Illinois and Northwestern University, where he research in theoretical astrophysics,star cluster, and numerical modeling.

IAN RIDPATH

Astronomy lecturer and writer ( University of London )

Ian Ridpath worked at the University of London Observatory until 1972, when he became a full-time writer, broadcaster, and lecturer on astronomy. A fellow of the Royal Astronomical Society, he edits the Oxford Dictionary of Astronomy and Norton's Star Atlas.

CAROLE STOTT
